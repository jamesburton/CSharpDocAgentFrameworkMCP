### Summary

Phase 14.1 fully achieves its goal. The integration gap between Phase 13 domain types and Phase 14 ingestion pipeline is closed:

1. **SolutionSnapshot population (GRAPH-01, GRAPH-03):** `IngestAsync` accumulates `ProjectEntry` records and `ProjectEdge` DAG from Roslyn's `ProjectReferences` and constructs a `SolutionSnapshot` with per-project `SymbolGraphSnapshot`s.

2. **Edge scope classification (GRAPH-02):** `ClassifyScope` is called at construction time inside `WalkTypeInline` — no post-classification pass. Edges correctly carry `EdgeScope.IntraProject`, `EdgeScope.CrossProject`, or `EdgeScope.External` based on assembly membership in the solution.

3. **Stub synthesis (GRAPH-04):** `MaybeAddStubNode` creates `NodeKind.Stub` nodes for external types (NuGet, .NET framework) not in the solution. A 30-entry primitive filter prevents noise from `System.String`, `System.Int32`, `Task`, etc.

4. **Stub filtering at index time (GRAPH-05):** Both `BM25SearchIndex` and `InMemorySearchIndex` filter to `NodeKind.Real` before populating their internal stores — stubs never enter `SearchAsync` or `GetAsync` results.

5. **Circular reference safety:** DFS cycle detector logs and adds warnings without removing edges or crashing.

All 5 claimed requirements (GRAPH-01 through GRAPH-05) are satisfied with both implementation evidence and passing tests.
